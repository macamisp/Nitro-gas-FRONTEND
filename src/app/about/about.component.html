Why Choose Us?

Commitment to Safety and Innovation Safety is a top priority for Nitro, with all operations following international standards to ensure customer well-being. In addition to safety, Nitro has embraced sustainable practices to minimize environmental impact. The company continues to innovate in energy solutions and improve its distribution and customer service networks, including initiatives like the Nitro Home Delivery app, which allows customers to order LPG cylinders directly to their doorstep

Visionary Team

Strategic Vision Nitro Gas Lanka aims to further expand its footprint and enhance energy security in Sri Lanka by investing in new technologies, improving infrastructure, and exploring sustainable energy options. As a government-owned entity, it is dedicated to supporting the country's energy goals, contributing to economic development, and maintaining its reputation as a trusted energy partner for millions of Sri Lankans. Nitro Gas Lanka is the primary supplier of Liquid Petroleum Gas (LPG) in Sri Lanka and a well-established household brand, NItro.
